My CISSP Journey: Passing at 100 Questions

Published on January 2026

Background

I work as a security generalist and work across cloud security, SIEM implementation, DevSecOps, risk assessment, compliance, and incident response. I already had hands-on experience in multiple CISSP domains, but topics like networking, physical security, and asset security were completely new for me.

I have 3.5 years of full-time experience and 1 year of internship experience, making it around 4.5 years in total. I also hold certifications such as CompTIA Security+, AWS Security Specialty, Google Professional Cloud Security Engineer, and Azure Security Engineer. With the one-year experience waiver from AWS Security Specialty, I meet the CISSP experience requirement.

Motivation and Starting Point

I was initially motivated to pursue CISSP by a colleague from my previous company. Later, my current manager, who also holds CISSP, motivated and supported me a lot throughout my journey.

While I did not start serious preparation immediately, I was passively consuming CISSP-related content from February 2025, spending around one to two hours per week.

The real preparation started in August 2025, when I purchased the official CISSP CBK and official practice tests.

Study Resources

Official CISSP CBK

I started studying from the official CISSP CBK but never completed it. I found the language difficult to understand, so I mostly used it as a reference and focused more on practice questions.

LinkedIn Learning - Mike Chapple

This course was the best resource for me. It explains concepts in a very simple and clear way and helps build the CISSP mindset. I completed the entire course in around two weeks.

Andrew Ramdayal

I purchased Andrew Ramdayal's CISSP course but completed only a small portion of it. I mainly watched the mindset videos and solved practice questions. By that time, I already felt prepared, but his mindset guidance was very useful.

Practice Tests

Practice tests played the most important role in my preparation.

  • Official CISSP practice tests including all domain-wise questions and four full-length tests
  • LearnZApp ISC2 Official CISSP App free questions
  • Destination CISSP App free questions
  • LinkedIn Learning full-length practice exams with scores around eighty percent

Exam Day Experience

I appeared for the exam at the Gurugram test center. Since there was no CISSP test center in Rajasthan, I had to travel almost six hundred kilometers.

The questions were scenario-based and similar to what I saw in the official CBK and LinkedIn Learning practice tests. I read each question carefully to identify keywords before selecting the answer.

The 100 Question Moment

When the exam suddenly ended at 100 questions and the survey appeared, I panicked. In the CISSP CAT format, the exam stops at 100 questions only if you perform very well or very poorly.

After completing the survey, I collected my belongings and waited for the result. When the printed result was handed to me and it said congratulations, it was an unforgettable moment.

CISSP Mindset and Strategy

Focus on keywords like BEST, FIRST, and PRIMARY. Always prioritize process over technology and follow the order prevent, detect, correct.

Policy implemented in Email security solution User training EDR Device encryption

In real life, you would apply multiple controls. In the CISSP exam, you must choose the single best preventive control. In this case, user training is the correct answer.

Final Thoughts

I am a hands-on security engineer and not a traditional manager. CISSP does not require you to be a manager.

If you understand how systems and company actually work, you can think like a manager.

Best of luck to everyone preparing for CISSP. Feel free to reach out to me on LinkedIn if you have any questions.
